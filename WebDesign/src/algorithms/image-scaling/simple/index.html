Image Scaling

Algorithm Overview

Scale your image by entering a scale ratio. The result uses smooth interpolation after applying bilinear rescaling.

How It Works

  • Upload an image.
  • Enter the scale ratio (e.g., 2 for double size, 0.5 for half size).
  • Click "Scale Image" to see and download the result.
